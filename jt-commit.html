Columbia Commit

Photo
Manipulation

I was asked to create this work for a friend of mine after he had committed to Columbia University to play football there.

This piece was created in October of 2020. I asked Justin what pictures he had in mind for this and after he had sent me to them I sketched out on paper a couple ideas on a layout. I also looked at the Columbia Football instagram to come up with something with the same theme.

This piece was created using Adobe Photoshop. I used layer masks to select the parts of the images that I like. I also used masks for the background to keep the city scape in the middle and then a concrete texture above and below that.
