WanderNational Parks

Frontend Development

I made this website as part of my Web I class that I took. Check out the site by either clicking the image or by clicking HERE.

P.S.- The only national park pathway that is fully developed is Grand Teton National Park

This website was made in may of 2022. During the course of my class I learned a lot of what I know now in terms of web development as far as sketching, using Figma, HTML, CSS, and a little bit of Javascript.

During the summer of 2021, my friends from high school and I went on a 4 week road trip and while we were researching for national parks to visit we ran into trouble looking for one service that would provide us with enough information about activities and things to do at these national parks. That being said, my motive behind creating this site was by creating one streamlined method for people looking for things to do at national parks where they can find trails, campsites, general lodging and more.
