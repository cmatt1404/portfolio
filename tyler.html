Tyler ConnSlam Dunk

Photo Manipulation

I created this for my friend who plays basketball at TCNJ. I enjoy doing graphics for sports, so I thought it would be cool to make this for him and I gave it to him for his birthday.

This work was made in March of 2022. Before creating this I went to one of Tyler's games and wanted to take photos for him not only because I think having pictures of yourself playing a sport is cool but also because I enjoy photographing and doing sports photogrpahy is interesting to me.

I used Adobe Photoshop for this work and as far as technique goes I selected pictures of Tyler from the ones that I took at his game, masked out the parts that I wanted to use. I have made works before with a glowing text affect before and I liked the way that it looked so I did the same thing here. For the background I had another picture of Tyler as well as a photo of a lion faded out.
