Senior Showcasepromotion

Graphic Design

I was asked to create a promotional graphic a showcase for our Interactive Multi-Media students. The graphic was asked to show elements from both our graphic design departments as well as interactive multi-media.

This piece was made in April of 2022. I made this piece by first sketching out the characters on the screen and then tracing and modifying them in Adobe Illustrator. The icons on the sides of the characters I found from a website called the noun project.
