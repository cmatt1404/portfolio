Hand Lettered Book Cover

Typographic Design

This was the final project for my typography class where the focus was creating a dust jacket for a book of our choosing using hand-lettered type. We spent time focusing on drawing out the typography and then converting it into a more refined digital copy.

This piece was made in December of 2022. The process for this piece was very involved across different media as well as different applications. First, I went and sketched out ideas on paper for what I wanted the front cover to look like. Next, I took my sketch into Illustrator to finalize the hand drawn typography as well as the background for the cover itself. Then, I went into Indesign and layed out all of the rest of the text that is seen on the back cover as well as the flaps that go around the book. The final step was printing and presenting the project.
