E-sports Jersey

Jersey Design

As part of the production team for the Gaming Club, I was tasked with created jerseys for our e-sports team that would set them apart from other teams.

This piece was created in March of 2022. I hadn't made a jersey design so I did some research as to what e-sports jerseys look like and get some inspiration.

After researching, I got designing and kept our school colors in mind with the gold and blue. The jagged lines on the arms and collar represent a scratch marks from a lion, and the topographic lines were included to add texture and depth to the jersey.
