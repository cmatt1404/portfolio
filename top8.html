Gaming ClubStandings

Social Media

One of the events that the gaming club would host would be bi-weekly tournaments of Super Smash Bros. It was my job to create graphics every two weeks to display the standings.

The first graphic in the slide was created in September of 2022 and the second graphic was made in February of 2022. Both of these were made as templates so it would be easy to update for each announcement of the winners of the tournaments.

As far as process goes, both of these pieces were made using Adobe Photoshop. The first graphic I used Mario as the placeholder because this was just a template at the time. During the weeks, I would edit the graphic with the appropriate renders as well as the usernames of the players, which is demonstrated in the second slide.
